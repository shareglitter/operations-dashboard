Core Blocks — Present Day Snapshot

Resident, Community, and Commercial blocks · Funded by neighbor subscribers

Block Tier Distribution
Tier 1 — Fully funded (weekly cleans), 5+ subs. The healthiest blocks.
Tier 2 — 4/4 cleans with less than 5 subs, or 2-3 cleans with 5+ subs. Nearly there.
Tier 3 — 2-3 cleans with less than 5 subs, or 1 clean per month. Growth opportunity.
Tier 4 — 0 Cleans, but has Pledges. Needs outreach to get to $50.
Tier 5 — 0 Cleans, but had previous cleans. Churned somewhere.
Tier 6 — 0 Cleans, 0 pledges. Dormant or new setup.
